How to Play Navaneeth's Word Game

1. Form Words: Drag your finger (or mouse) over adjacent letters to form words. You can only use each letter tile once per word. Release to submit.

2. Find All Words: Your goal is to find all the hidden words displayed in the empty boxes above the letter wheel.

3. Use Hints: Click the "Reveal Letter" button, then click an empty square to reveal one letter. Use hints wisely!

4. Progress Through Levels: Complete levels to unlock the "Next Level" button, or use "Select Level" to jump to any puzzle.

Note: Every word in this game is at least 3 letters long.
